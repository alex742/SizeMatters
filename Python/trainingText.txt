This dissertation project looks in detail at an aspect of this type of reasoning, inparticular the general sizes of objects. Humans can estimate the approximate sizes of avast range of objects having not seen them in recent memory. This dissertation has theaim of using natural language processing to extract information from web pages in order to create a database spanning all common 'objects' (inclusive of animals; vegetation;buildings and other inanimate physical entities) with their approximate measured sizes.
